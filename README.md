## Technical Approach

**Prompt Engineering Principles:**
- Domain-specific constraints to reduce hallucination
- Explicit uncertainty handling (speculation marked, assumptions tested)
- Source-anchored outputs (citations, jurisdictional relevance)
- Human-in-the-loop design (tools assist, humans decide)

**Core Competencies:**
- Legal reasoning & statutory interpretation
- Cognitive systems design
- AI quality control & fact-checking
- Structured decision frameworks
